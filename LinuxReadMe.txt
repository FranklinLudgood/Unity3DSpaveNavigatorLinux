In order to us a 3d Mouse in Unity3d on a Linux Computer you need to confirm to these 2 things.
1. The driver I used is called Spacenav it can found at Spacenav.sourceforge.net. This is a free and effective alternative the 3DConnexion device driver.
2. The library I used libspnav.so is a shared library built on Linux Mint with a x86-64 bit processor.
